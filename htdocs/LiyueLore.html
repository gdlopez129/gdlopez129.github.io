Lore


THE CITY OF CONTRACTS

Chinese:「 Liyue / "璃月" 」

————————————————————————
"The God of Contracts, senselessly slaughtered as his people watched on in horror. In the end, he will sign the contract to end all contracts."
————————————————————————
Liyue is the nation that prospers under the rule of the oldest and most powerful archon in Teyvat: the God of Contracts. The bloody origin of Liyue's cold and merciless archon is known far and wide all across Teyvat. Yet despite this, it is common knowledge among Liyuen folk that he was a ruler who deeply cared about his people.
