Chests


NOTICE!: Hello dear Traveler! Since you're probably new to Genshin Impact, Teyvat Map for Dummies would like to present you with a very special gift: the location of 3 Luxurious chests. Chests, even Common ones, are very valuable as you rank up in the game, so knowing the locations of some chests with the highest value will surely be of some help to you!

Chest Locations:

Chest 1:

The first chest is above the Knights of Favonius Headquarters. Teyvat Map for Dummies would suggest that you climb using the brick wall right beside the Headquarters' doors (you'll find it easily, trust us)

Chest 2:

The second chest is right above the Favonius Cathedral. Teyvat Map for Dummies would like to suggest that you climb using one of the archways that support the Cathedral walls. Once you manage to climb up, use the bell tower to reach the chest.

Chest 3:

The third chest is easy to find. It's right down a big waterfall, so you'll have no trouble spotting it.
