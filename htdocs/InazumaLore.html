Lore


THE NATION OF ETERNITY

Japanese:「 Inazuma / "璃月" 」

————————————————————————
"What do mortals see of the eternity chased after by their god?"
————————————————————————
Under the rule of the God of Eternity, Inazuma is a nation that's isolated from all others. In the perspective of their god,"Eternity is closest unto Heaven"; solidifying the god's will to eliminate anything in her path that could pose as a potential threat in her pursuit of the unchanging.
