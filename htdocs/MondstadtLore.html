Lore


THE CITY OF FREEDOM

German:「 Mondstadt / "Moon City" 」

————————————————————————
"What does freedom really mean when demanded of you by a god?"
————————————————————————
The countries in Teyvat are typically ruled by gods that are otherwise known as "archons". But unlike the rest, the city of Mondstadt lives in peace without one.

According to legends, it is said that the God of Wind cast a feather loose as a symbol of freedom upon his land. Yet the question remains: what if there was more to the matter than what was written in ancient texts?
