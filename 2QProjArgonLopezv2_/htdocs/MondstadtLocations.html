Locations


The City of Wind: Mondstadt


The wind will carry dandelion seeds, songs, and stories far afield, and it also guides gentle Travelers to its realm. Welcome to Mondstadt.

The Marsh of Celestial Guardians: Starfell Lake


A poem says that a star once fell to the earth, forming this lake. But other tales say that even the stars that shine as bright as the Anemo Archon's eyes will fall into this lake.

Windswept Wilderness: Windrise


This plain bathes in an everlasting breeze. An ancient, monumental tree left behind by an ancient heroine speaks softly amidst the winds.

Land of Clear Springs: Springvale


A peaceful and idyllic suburban town. It has a long history of hunting, of sweet spring water, and of passing down the legend of a beneficent spirit dwelling within the waters.

Manor of Daybreak: Dawn Winery


Filled with the fragrance of grapes and wine, this is the estate of one of Mondstadt's distinguished houses. It has conquered drinkers of the city over with its unmatched wines, and is in some ways the holy land to which all who love the bottle must make pilgrimage.
